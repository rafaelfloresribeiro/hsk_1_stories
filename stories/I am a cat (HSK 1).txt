
			
In this graded reader, you will get to know Xiao Mao.



Mandarin



我是一只猫



我是一只猫。



我叫小猫。



我喜欢人。



我不喜欢狗。



再见，我去睡觉了。







Multiple Choice Questions







English Translation



I am a cat



I am a cat.



My name is Xiao Mao.



I like people.



I don’t like dogs.



Goodbye, I am going to sleep.



Vocabulary




我 (wǒ) – I, me



是 (shì) – to be



一只 (yì zhī) – one (measure word for animals)



猫 (māo) – cat



叫 (jiào) – to be called, to call



小猫 (xiǎo māo) – little cat, kitten



喜欢 (xǐhuān) – to like



人 (rén) – person, people



不 (bù) – not, no



狗 (gǒu) – dog



再见 (zàijiàn) – goodbye



去 (qù) – to go



睡觉 (shuìjiào) – to sleep
