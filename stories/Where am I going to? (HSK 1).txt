
			
In this little graded reader, Xiao Di tells you which places he is going to visit today.



Mandarin



我去了哪里？



你好，我叫小迪。



今天上午，我去了医院。



今天中午，我去了商店。



今天下午，我去饭店吃了饭。



现在，我在家看电视。







Multiple Choice Questions







English Translation



Where did I go?



Hello. My name is Xiao Di.



This morning, I went to the hospital.



This afternoon, I went to the store.



This evening, I went to a restaurant to eat.



Right now, I am watching TV at home.



Vocabulary




我 (wǒ) – I, me



去 (qù) – to go



了 (le) – (indicates past action)



哪里 (nǎlǐ) – where



你好 (nǐ hǎo) – hello



叫 (jiào) – to be called, to call



小迪 (Xiǎo Dí) – (name) Little Di



今天 (jīntiān) – today



上午 (shàngwǔ) – morning



医院 (yīyuàn) – hospital



中午 (zhōngwǔ) – noon



商店 (shāngdiàn) – shop, store



下午 (xiàwǔ) – afternoon



饭店 (fàndiàn) – restaurant, hotel



吃 (chī) – to eat



饭 (fàn) – meal, rice



现在 (xiànzài) – now



家 (jiā) – home, house



看 (kàn) – to watch, to look at



电视 (diànshì) – television
