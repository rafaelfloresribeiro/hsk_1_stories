
			
He Hua tells you about the weather in China and the United States.



Mandarin



你好，我的名字叫何华。



我是中国人。



我住在中国。



我说汉语。



现在中国的天气很好。



美国的天气也很好。







Multiple Choice Questions







English Translation



Hello. My name is He Hua.



I am Chinese.



I live in China.



I speak Chinese.



The weather in China is very nice right now.



The weather in the United States is also very nice.



Vocabulary




你好 (nǐ hǎo) – hello



我的 (wǒ de) – my, mine



名字 (míngzì) – name



叫 (jiào) – to be called, to call



何华 (Hé Huá) – (name) He Hua



是 (shì) – to be



中国人 (Zhōngguó rén) – Chinese person



住 (zhù) – to live



在 (zài) – at, in, on



汉语 (Hànyǔ) – Chinese (language)



现在 (xiànzài) – now



天气 (tiānqì) – weather



很好 (hěn hǎo) – very good, fine



美国 (Měiguó) – United States
